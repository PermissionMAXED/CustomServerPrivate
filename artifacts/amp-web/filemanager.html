Convert to OIDC

                                
Converts this user to OIDC authentication. Their password will be cleared and they will only be able to log in via the configured OIDC provider.

                                
                            

                            
                            
                            

                                
Revert to Local Authentication

                                
Reverts this user back to local (password-based) authentication. Their OIDC link will be removed and their password will be cleared. A password reset will be required before they can log in again.
